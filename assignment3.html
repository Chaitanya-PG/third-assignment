She was very good in her studies with her parents' help. She made lots of friends there. But since the friend circle increased hence she was not concentrating much on her studies hence her parents transferred her to another school for her ninth which is "Greenfields Public School". She did her matriculation from that school and further got transferred to a new school "Taksila Public School" from where she completed her Intermediate level.

With all the college memories and friends she then took her next step of working in a reputed company.

She started working in a company named "Mphasis Pvt. Ltd." She worked there for sometime and in the mean time came a marriage proposal and as all went well, she got married on 24th April 2015. Soon after the marriage she relocated to an international location "Dubai". She lived there for three years and also gave birth to a beautiful baby and named him "Hrithvik K C". She worked in Dubai in a passport and visa company working for the Indian Embassy, "BLS International". She now returned back to Delhi and started working in "The Yogshala" as a Public Relations Officer. She is leading a happy and wonderful life today.
